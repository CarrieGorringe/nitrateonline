Gianni Truzzi

In addition to Nitrate Online, Gianni Truzzi's reviews and features can be read regularly in Seattle Weekly, along with freelance contributions to other publications.

Gianni has earned degrees in both Theater and Computer Science, and worked as an actor and software engineer before pursuing the writer's craft.

He lives in Seattle with his wife and daughter.
