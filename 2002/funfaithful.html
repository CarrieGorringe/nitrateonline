Unfaithful

Paula Nechak and Cynthia Fuchs review the new film directed by Adrian Lyne , Unfaithful.

  • Unfaithful - Unfaithful is mature and sensibly, if frustratingly, smart. - Paula Nechak.
  • Unfaithful - Unfaithful quickly descends into a murky moral relativity, where obsession substitutes for love and women forgive all. - Cynthia Fuchs.
