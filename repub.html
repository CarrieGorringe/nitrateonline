Nitrate Online offers several ways to host our content:

Linking/Framing

This program is geared to websites who want to host content from Nitrate Online at minimal expense. Under this program:

  • You may add links to content from Nitrate Online or create a frameset that references content from Nitrate Online.
  • You may not physically host the content on your website - any time a user accesses the content, it must be rendered by our server.
  • You may not republish the content to other websites or print publications.

There is no charge for this service. We do ask, however, that you notify us of content to which you have linked.

Web Republishing

This program is geared to websites that want to host content from Nitrate Online, but add their own branding. Under this program,

  • You may physically host content from Nitrate Online on your website, reformatting as needed.
  • You must include a byline as described below.
  • You must include the copyright no
  • Any edits to the content must be approved by Nitrate Online.
  • You may not republish the content to other websites or print publications.

There is a nominal monthly fee for this service, based on the traffic level of your website.

Print Republishing

This program is geared to print publications that want to publish content from Nitrate Online. Under this program:

  • You may publish content from Nitrate Online in your newspaper or magazine, reformatting as needed.
  • You must include a byline as described below.
  • You must include the copyright notice described below.
  • Any edits to the content must be approved by Nitrate Online.
  • You may not republish the content to other websites or print publications.

There is a nominal  fee for this service, based on your circulation.

Byline

For Web Republishing and Print Republishing, you must include a byline on the content as follows:

by Author Name
Nitrate Online (www.nitrateonline.com)

Copyright Notice

For Web Republishing and Print Republishing, you must include the copyright notice found at the bottom of the content on Nitrate Online.
