Cynthia Fuchs

Cynthia Fuchs is an associate professor of English, film & media studies, African American studies, and cultural studies at George Mason University, as well as a regular film, media, and book reviewer for the Philadelphia Citypaper, popmatters.com and reelimagesmagazine.com. She is the author of a variety of articles in books and journals, on topics ranging from Vietnam War, cyborg, porn, and interracial buddy movies, to Michael Jackson, the Artist, and Madonna, to grunge, queer punks, and hiphop.
